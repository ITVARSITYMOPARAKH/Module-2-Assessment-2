About Pine City Mall

Welcome to Pine City Mall, your ultimate destination for shopping, dining, and fun! Explore 150+ stores, from trendy fashion at ZARA to gourmet treats at Haagen-Dazs, and enjoy a wide variety of restaurants, cafés, and entertainment options.

Our modern, spacious layout makes every visit enjoyable, whether you’re shopping, catching a movie, or spending quality time with family. Pine City Mall is more than a mall—it’s a place to discover, relax, and connect.

Fun Fact: We host seasonal events and special promotions throughout the year to make every visit exciting!
